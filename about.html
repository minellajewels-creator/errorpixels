The Artist

Meet Mr. Error

Every great story needs a passionate narrator. Mr. Error — the creative force behind Error Pixels — found his calling behind the lens, where the ordinary becomes extraordinary and imperfection tells the most honest stories.

Based in Coimbatore, Mr. Error has spent years honing the craft of visual storytelling across weddings, cultural events, fashion shoots, and brand campaigns. Each project is approached not just as a job, but as a creative collaboration — understanding your vision and bringing it to life with artistry.

"I don't just capture moments — I capture the feeling that lives inside them. That's what makes Error Pixels different."

— Mr. Error, Founder

The Philosophy

Art in Every Frame

The name "Error Pixels" isn't a mistake — it's a statement. We believe the most beautiful images come from embracing the unexpected: the candid tear, the spontaneous laugh, the golden ray of light that wasn't planned.

Our approach blends cinematic technique with documentary honesty. We don't stage your memories — we elevate them. Every image is post-processed with care, preserving authenticity while delivering a premium, editorial-quality result.

From the grandest wedding venues to the most intimate family moments — we bring the same passion, professionalism, and artistic eye to every shoot.
