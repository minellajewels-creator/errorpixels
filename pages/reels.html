Adding reels is simple — just paste Instagram embed codes below each reel slot in the HTML file:

01

02

Copy the embed code. It looks like: <blockquote class="instagram-media" ...>

03

Open pages/reels.html and paste inside a .reel-slot div, replacing the placeholder.
